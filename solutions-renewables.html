Powering a Sustainable Future

VoltEdge is at the forefront of Africa's renewable energy transformation. We deliver turnkey solar PV solutions, advanced battery energy storage systems (BESS), and hybrid power plants that reduce carbon emissions while providing reliable, affordable electricity.

From feasibility studies to EPC project delivery and long-term operations, our team brings deep expertise in utility-scale renewables, helping our clients achieve energy independence and sustainability goals.

Our Renewable Services

Utility-Scale Solar PV

Complete EPC services for solar farms from 1MW to 100MW+, including design, procurement, construction, and grid interconnection.

  • Site assessment & yield analysis
  • Module & inverter selection
  • Civil & electrical installation
  • Grid connection & commissioning

Battery Energy Storage

Advanced BESS solutions for grid stability, peak shaving, and renewable time-shifting to maximize clean energy utilization.

  • Lithium-ion & flow battery systems
  • Energy management systems
  • Grid ancillary services
  • Microgrid integration

Hybrid & Microgrids

Integrated solar + storage + diesel hybrid solutions for mining sites, remote communities, and industrial facilities.

  • Hybrid system design & optimization
  • Controller & SCADA integration
  • Remote monitoring & O&M
  • Diesel reduction strategies
